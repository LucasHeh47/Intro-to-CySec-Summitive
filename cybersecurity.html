Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is defending and protecting computers, and preventing damage to a computer via cyberattacks.

Examples

Developers have to find all the vulnerabilities possible and fix them in order to decrease the chance of being hacked. Ethical hackers hack into the computer with permission and find ways to improve on security.
