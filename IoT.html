IoT

(Internet of Things)

Network of Physical Objects Industrial and communal settings.

Examples

  • Google Home
  • Amazon Alexa
  • Doorbell Cameras
  • Smart Light Switches

Vulnerabilities

  • Weak or hardcoded passwords

    • Weak or hardcoded passwords are the easiest way for hackers to compromise IoT devices.

  • Use of Insecure or Outdated Components

    • The use of outdated or insecure software, including open-source components by manufacturers to build their IoT devices creates a complex supply chain that is difficult to track.

  • Lack of Secure Update Mechanism

    • A corrupted update can disrupt the operations of critical IoT devices and have physical consequences in sectors like the healthcare or energy.
